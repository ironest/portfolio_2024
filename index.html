Yes, it's me!

My official name is Riccardo Carzaniga, but everyone calls me Ricky. I was born and raised in Italy, close to the city of Milan.

The 16,000+ kilometre migration

Approximately a decade ago, I departed from my homeland (Italy) and embarked on a journey to Australia. During my initial seven years as a migrant, I resided in the captivating city of Sydney, situated in the New South Wales state. Primarily driven by educational pursuits and career opportunities, I immersed myself in the vibrant Sydney lifestyle. Two years ago, I once again packed my belongings and relocated to Queensland, where I have been living ever since.

Rollerblading roller_skating

I don't really do much sport. But rollerblading is my go to. It's great for building balance, strengthening muscles and boosting mental health. It's also a cheap, fun, and eco-friendly way to get your daily cardio in. In other words, rollerblading has it all favorite
